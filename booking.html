Terms and conditions of hire

Equipment

The following items of equipment are included in the hire package (dependent on package and selected games):

  • Bean Bag Toss (2 wooden boards + 6 bean bags)
  • Big Game Hunters "Big 4" (Giant 4-in-row game including 4ft high frame + 42 counters)
  • Big Game Hunters "Full Size Croquet Set" (4 mallets, 4 balls, 6 hoops, 1 winning post)
  • Big Game Hunters "Giant Hi-Tower" (Giant block stacking game including 58 wooden blocks, starting height 3ft 6in)
  • Big Game Hunters "Wooden Boules Set" (8 polished wooden boules, 1 measurer, 1 wooden jack)
  • Big Game Hunters "Wooden Garden Skittles" (9 x 23cm skittles + 3 regulation balls)
  • Ginger Ray Giant Noughts & Crosses (1mx1m rope grid + 10 wooden shapes)
  • Jacques of London Giant Dominoes (28 black wooden dominoes, each 7 inches in length)
  • Limbo (2 x 5ft timber frames + pole)
  • Tin Can Alley (6 tins + 2 bean bags + crate)
  • Wooden Quoits game (wooden cross, 5 posts, 4 rings)
  • Wooden chalkboard + pens (customisable chalkboard which can be used as a sign for the lawn games)

Rent and deposit

The total hire cost is:

  • Platimum package: £150 for 10 games
  • Gold package: £125 for 8 games
  • Silver package: £100 for 6 games

All packages include wooden chalkboard sign and pens.

Where applicable (within 20 miles of Newport, South Wales), delivery, set-up and collection will be provided at no extra cost.

An additional £50 damage deposit is also required, which will be refunded at the end of the hire period provided the items are returned in good condition.

To secure a booking we require a booking deposit of 50% of the full booking fee, which is non-refundable in the event of cancellation by the Hirer. The remaining balance (remainder of hire fee + £50 damage deposit) must be paid no later than 14 days before the event date.

Loss and damage

The equipment should only be used outdoors in dry conditions, or with sufficient cover to prevent water damage.

In the event of any damage to the equipment (beyond usual wear-and-tear), the Hirer is responsible for the costs of any necessary repairs. In the unlikely event of the equipment being lost or damaged beyond repair, the Hirer is responsible for the costs of replacing the items as set out below:

  • Bean Bag Toss: Wooden board £75 each; Bean bags £2 each
  • Big Game Hunters "Big 4": Frame £160; Individual counters £3 each
  • Big Game Hunters "Full Size Croquet Set": Mallet £20 each; Ball £10 each; Hoop £10 each; Winning post £15
  • Big Game Hunters "Giant Hi-Tower": £3 per block, up to a maximum of £75
  • Big Game Hunters "Wooden Boules Set": Boules £5 each; Jack £5; Measurer £5
  • Big Game Hunters "Wooden Garden Skittles": Skittles £10 each; Balls £5 each
  • Ginger Ray Giant Noughts & Crosses: £20
  • Jacques of London Giant Dominoes: £30
  • Limbo: £110
  • Tin Can Alley: £20
  • Wooden chalkboard: £30
  • Wooden Quoits game: £15

For any damages or losses amounting to less than £50, the costs will be taken from the damage deposit and the remainder returned to the Hirer. For any damages or losses amounting to more than £50, the Hirer will be responsible for meeting the additional costs beyond £50.
